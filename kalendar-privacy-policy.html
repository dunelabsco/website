Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

1. Introduction

Kalendar is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (collected via Apple Sign-In)
  • Display name (collected via Apple Sign-In)

Your Content

  • Tasks and calendar entries you create
  • Chat messages you send to the AI assistant
  • Voice recordings (temporary, for AI transcription)

Usage Information

  • Device information (for crash reporting via Crashlytics)
  • App version and usage patterns

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the app
  • Send you notifications and reminders
  • Process your subscription
  • Process your AI assistant requests
  • Fix problems and improve the app
  • Comply with legal requirements

4. Data Protection

Your data is protected with industry-standard security measures. We encrypt data in transit and at rest. You have full control over your data and can delete it at any time.

5. Third-Party Services

Our app uses third-party services for core functionality. These services have their own privacy policies:

Apple

Purpose: Authentication (Apple Sign-In) and in-app purchases

Privacy Policy: apple.com/privacy

Google Firebase

Purpose: Cloud storage, authentication, and crash reporting

Services: Firestore (database), Firebase Auth, Firebase Functions, Crashlytics

Privacy Policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Google Gemini AI

Purpose: AI assistant functionality

What we share: Your chat messages and voice recordings are sent to Google Gemini AI to process your requests and provide AI assistant features

Voice recordings: Transcribed by Gemini AI and not stored after processing

Chat messages: Processed by Gemini AI to understand your requests and generate responses

Data transmission: All data is sent securely via encrypted connections through our Cloud Functions

Privacy Policy: ai.google.dev/gemini-api-privacy

Consent: By using the AI assistant features, you consent to this data sharing with Google Gemini AI

We do not share your personal information with other third parties without your consent.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your data (export it in JSON format via Profile → Download Data)
  • Delete your account and all associated data (Profile → Delete Account)
  • Change your privacy settings at any time
  • Withdraw from using certain features (you can stop using AI features at any time)

7. Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as you have an account. Once you delete your account, all your data is permanently removed from our servers and third-party services.

Voice recordings are temporary and are not stored after transcription by Gemini AI.

8. Children

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email.

10. Contact

Email: kalendar@dunelabs.co
